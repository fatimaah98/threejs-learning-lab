## ğŸ§± Textures Used

This lesson uses three main texture types:

### 1. Color Map (`map`)

Defines the base color of the surface.

### 2. Normal Map (`normalMap`)

Adds surface detail by simulating small bumps and dents without increasing geometry complexity.

### 3. Roughness Map (`roughnessMap`)

Controls how shiny or matte different areas of the surface are.
